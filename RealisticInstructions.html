Realistic Task Instructions

Welcome to the interactive part of the user study! On the next page, you'll be making your own pizzas. You'll need to find the correct ingredients to fulfill the order given to you. Each ingredient belongs to its own category, e.g. pepperoni is found in the protein category.

As you make work through pizza orders, you'll encounter different designs that may make it harder or easier to assemble your pizzas. Have a think about how Fitts' Law is affecting your workflow as you try to make the perfect pizza.
